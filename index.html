I am a PhD candidate in Public Policy at The University of Chicago

‍

I am an applied microeconomist and my research revolve around the study of firms and schools responses to government regulations, and their effects on the sorting of individuals into workplaces and schools.

‍

More broadly, my interests revolve around labor economics, educational policy, and their inequality and efficiency consequences.

‍

If you're interested in talking about my work, or yours, get in touch! You can reach me at descobarsalce@uchicago.edu
